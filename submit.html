About Land Surveys

A certified land survey is the most objective documentation you can provide. It establishes:

  • Exact legal boundary lines
  • Corner stakes & monuments (iron pins, concrete monuments)
  • Easements & rights-of-way
  • Encroachments & encumbrances
  • Topography & elevation (if ALTA/NSPS survey)
